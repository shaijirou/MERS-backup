Privacy Policy for MERS App

Last updated: October 28, 2025

The MERS App is developed to assist in monitoring and reporting emergencies within the Municipality of Agoncillo. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide such as name, contact number, and address.
  • Device location when enabled by the user for reporting and tracking purposes.
  • Uploaded photos, IDs, or reports related to incidents.

How We Use the Information

  • To verify user identity and account information.
  • To process and respond to emergency reports.
  • To improve safety and communication within the Municipality of Agoncillo.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no system is 100% secure, and users should exercise caution when sharing information.

Permissions Used

  • Location: For tracking and mapping incident locations.
  • Camera: For capturing and uploading report evidence.
  • Storage: For saving and retrieving uploaded files.
